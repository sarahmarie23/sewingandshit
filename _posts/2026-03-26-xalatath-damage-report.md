# Damage Done

This post serves as a space for me to jot down where I took damage to various pieces of the costume, opportunities for upgrades, and to give credit for quality craftsmanship. The following documentation lists out each piece with numerical ratings and descriptions highlighting the positives, notifying what changes must be made, and opportunities to make this even better.

# Rating System

## Accuracy 

**3 points** - Xal'atath would be pleased at my work

**2 points** - Clearly reads as the character, but some minor changes could improve it	

**1 point** - Either only partially accurate or would need major changes to match the design better					

## Comfort

**3 points** - Comfortable to wear; easy to put on and move in	

**2 points** - Wearable, but somewhat bothersome or restrictive

**1 point** - Required assistance to put on and/or was annoying or difficult to wear	

## Repair

**5 points** - Perfect as is

**4 points** - Wearable today, but could use improvements or reinforcement		

**3 points** - Needs minor fixes before next wear					

**2 ponts** - Major repairs required before it can be worn again

**1 point** - Missing or damaged beyond repair; must be remade

# MY OBSERVATIONS (from Head to Toe)

## Wig

**Accuracy:** 2  
**Comfort:** 3  
**Repair:** 4  

### What went well
- Color is good.
- The wig works well with my upgraded wig cap and stays on in wind and movement.
- It's attractive and the side part covering more of the left side works.

### Changes I MUST make
- None (maybe just wash it before the next event).

### Ideas for improvements
- Remove some wefts so the wig isn't so full.
- Trim it to remove the messy ends and make it shorter for accuracy.

## Ears

**Accuracy:** 2  
**Comfort:** 1  
**Repair:** 2  

### What went well
- Size is accurate.
- Earrings worked.
- The colors match the face makeup.

### Changes I MUST make
- Find a way to make them securely stay on my head.
- Figure out how to seal it so the makeup doesn't peel off.

### Ideas for improvements
