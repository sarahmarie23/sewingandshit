## ðŸ§  Shit That Lived in My Head

- I used up 2 whole sheets of foam and still need more.

- Silver sharpie for tracing foam is a game changer.

- Painting on multiple layers is so tedious! Mod Podge x2, black paint x2, gold paint x2 or as needed, varnish (x2 on front, x1 on back). You're supposed to wait at least 24 hours for the paint to dry before varnish, and then 24 hours between layers.
